## Important Name Check

An established macOS Homebrew utility already uses the name Cork and the domain [corkmac.app](https://corkmac.app/). Its product category is different, but the shared Mac platform creates meaningful App Store, search, and customer-confusion risk.

Before submitting Cork's final App Store name:

1. Confirm the name is accepted in App Store Connect.
2. Search current U.S. and intended international trademark records for software-related conflicts.
3. Consider a qualified store name such as `Cork: Ambient Corkboard` if Apple accepts it.
4. Consider a more distinctive product name before launch if legal or storefront checks raise concerns.

This is a practical naming warning, not legal advice. A trademark professional can assess the actual risk for the countries where the app will be sold.
